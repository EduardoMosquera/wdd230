Wedding Reception Accommodations

Facilities and Capacity

You can accommodate up to 350 guests at our hotels next to the temple. We have options for rehearsal dinners and smaller, intimate gatherings of up to 50 people. Our Grand Mainroom features floor-to-ceiling windows, teardrop chandeliers, tall ceilings and a gorgeously draped bay window. For smaller affairs, we offer our Junior Staroom. If you prefer an outdoor celebration, we also offer their beautiful Courtyard with a romantic gazebo.

Services Offered

We offer a full-service wedding specialist to ensure your vision comes to life. We offer event planning as well as event rentals to make sure you have everything you need for your big day. Additional services include:

  • Setup
  • Cleanup
  • Tables
  • Chairs

  • Lighting and sound
  • Accommodations
